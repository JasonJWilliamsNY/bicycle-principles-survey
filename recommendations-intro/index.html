Recommendation Descriptive FeaturesðŸ”—

Every recommendation has six descriptive features

  • Summary: Expounds upon what this is, including what problem this tries to solve, and evidence this could work.
  • How might this work: Brief example of how this could be implemented and how might success be evaluated
  • Related Principles: Listing of relevant Bicycle principles
  • Benefits to the learners: How this helps learners (directly or indirectly)
  • Incentives to Implementers: What motivates implementers to enact this recommendation
  • Barriers to Implementation: What obstacles prevent this recommendation from being enacted
